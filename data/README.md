# Data Directory

This folder contains all datasets used in the Paris Case Study.
Each subfolder corresponds to one section of the analysis notebook.


## 1_Historical_trends_to_quantify_the_problem
- `indicateurs_icu.csv` : Dataset describing Urban Heat Island indicators for Paris, including variables such as vegetation density, building height, and maximum temperature. *Source: Météo-France (2024).*
- `temperature_moyenne_paris.csv` : Historical annual mean temperatures in Paris from 1873 to 2024. *Source: Météo-France (2024).*



## 2_Climate_environmental_projections_for_the_region
- `Paris_TRACC_2030.csv` : Climate projections for Paris under future scenarios (TRACC model) with expected changes in average temperature and precipitation. *Source: Copernicus Climate Data Store (2024).*
- `espaces_verts.csv` : Dataset on green space distribution and vegetation cover across Paris used to analyze the relationship between vegetation and local temperature. *Source: City of Paris & Copernicus (2023).*



## 3_Socioeconomic_data
- `paris_income_by_arrondissement.csv` : Median income by arrondissement in Paris, used to measure socioeconomic vulnerability to heat exposure. *Source: INSEE (2023).*
- `populations-legales-communes-et-arrondissements-municipaux-millesime-ile-de-fran.csv` : Official 2020 population counts for each arrondissement in Paris. *Source: INSEE (2023).*


## 4_Cost-benefit_analysis_of_adaptation_measures
- `evaluation-climat-du-budget-d-ela-collectivite.csv` : Evaluation of the climate budget and estimated costs and benefits of local adaptation measures (e.g., green roofs, cooling infrastructure).  
  *Source: City of Paris (2022).*


All data are stored locally to ensure full reproducibility of the analysis.
